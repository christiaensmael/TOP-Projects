Salmon Fish Finger Sandwich

Description

Homemade gourmet salmon fish finger sandwich with a good dollop of tangy tatar sauce.

Ingredients

  • 3 tblsp plain flour
  • 1 large egg
  • 100g dried breadcrumbs
  • 4 skinless and boneless salmon fillets
  • 2-3 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 little Gem lettuce
  • 4 sub rolls
  • 5 tbsp mayonaise
  • 1 heaped tbsp capers
  • 2 Spring onions
  • 1/2 small pack tarragon
  • Juice 1/2 lemon
